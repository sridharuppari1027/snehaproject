Vision

To create a safer and more secure society by empowering the youth through skill development, security training, and meaningful employment opportunities in the security and surveillance sectors.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between unemployment and career growth by providing comprehensive security training, skill development, and certification to young individuals, enabling them to secure stable jobs in security services, hospitals, shopping malls, and PSO roles — contributing to national safety and community well-being.

About Us

At Shiloh Sneha Security Services Private Limited, we are committed to safeguarding what matters most. Founded in April 2025 and headquartered in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, our company was built on the principles of trust, vigilance, and professionalism. We specialize in comprehensive security and investigation services, offering tailored solutions to meet the evolving needs of residential, commercial, and institutional clients.Led by directors Sneha Latha Ashok Mathangi and Kavya Preethi Mathangi, our team brings a proactive approach to security management, combining modern surveillance techniques with a deep understanding of local safety dynamics. Whether it’s manned guarding, event security, or risk assessment, we deliver peace of mind through precision and integrity.
